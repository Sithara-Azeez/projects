UBTAN FACE PACK

What is Ubtan?

If you haven’t come across this name before, let me take you through it. In many traditions, especially in North India, this is considered as one of the oldest and sacred form of cosmetic that was ever discovered. The goodness of this au-natural recipe is because of its simple yet very powerful ingredients. Ubtan is known as a magical Ayurvedic recipe to detox your skin from head to toe. It is believed that Ubtan removes all the dead cells and prepares for the new. There are many ways to prepare Ubtan but I have picked up ingredients that are mostly suitable for all skin types and aids almost all kinds of minor skin troubles. Not just women, even men can totally rely on this one

Ingredients used:

  • Gram flour/ Besan
  • Red sandalwood powder
  • Red lentil/ Masoor dal powder
  • Wild turmeric powder/ Kasturi Manjal and
  • Few other natural elements
The unique blend of these ingredients softens and smoothens your skin while effectively cleansing and exfoliating it.

Main benefits of the Ubtan pack are:

  1. Radiant complexion
  2. Removes tan
  3. Treats acne
  4. Fades blemishes
  5. Exfoliates dead skin
  6. Evens skin tone
  7. Reduces dark circles
  8. Soothes dry skin
  9. Removes facial hair
Take a small quantity of this powder and mix it well with either curd, milk or rose water according to your skin type.
It can be used as a weekly face pack around 2-3 times in a week or on a daily basis instead of soap or face wash as well. Do try. It’s a wonderful promising pack.
